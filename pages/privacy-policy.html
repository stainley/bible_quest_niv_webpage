Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains how Bible Quest NIV collects, uses, and protects information.

Last Updated: June 2026


Bible Quest NIV ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected when you use the App.



1. Information We Collect


Usage and Analytics Data

The App may collect anonymous usage information through services such as Google Firebase Analytics.

  • App interactions and feature usage
  • Reading activity and engagement metrics
  • Trivia participation and completion statistics
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App performance information
  • General geographic region (country or region level)

Purpose: This information is used strictly to improve functionality, content, and user experience.



Crash Reports

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect information about crashes and technical issues.

  • Device information
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Technical logs related to crashes

Purpose: Helps identify and resolve software issues.



Reading Data Stored on Device

  • Reading progress
  • Bookmarks
  • Notes and highlights
  • Reading streaks and statistics
  • Trivia progress and scores
  • User preferences and settings

This information is primarily stored locally on your device.



2. Special App Features


QR Code Verse Sharing

Bible Quest NIV allows users to generate QR codes containing Bible references and verse content.

  • No personal information is embedded.
  • No recipient tracking occurs.
  • No scan tracking occurs.
  • Users control what content they share.

Trivia Features

The App may record:

  • Questions answered
  • Quiz completion status
  • Scores and achievements
  • Participation statistics


3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain App functionality
  • Track reading progress and achievements
  • Improve features and user experience
  • Optimize performance
  • Develop new updates
  • Fix bugs and technical issues


4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

The App may utilize:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google Play Services


5. Children's Privacy

Bible Quest NIV is intended for a general audience and is safe for families, students, and children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.



6. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

However, no method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed 100% secure.



7. Your Choices

  • You may uninstall the App at any time.
  • You may control analytics preferences through your device or Google account settings where available.


8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any revised Privacy Policy.
